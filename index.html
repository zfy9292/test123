关于我

About

在学术研究、产业实践与学生培养之间建立连接

Connecting academic research, industry practice, and student development

赵奉禹，博士，讲师，中级经济师，现任西南林业大学经济管理学院工商管理系教师。研究与教学关注工商管理领域中的国际商贸、电子商务、跨文化管理、数字经济与AI商业创新等议题。个人经历兼具学术研究与企业实践，曾参与或主持多项科研与商业项目，并在创新创业赛事指导与实践教学方面积累了较丰富经验。

Fengyu Zhao is a Ph.D., Lecturer, and Intermediate Economist at the Department of Business Administration, College of Economics and Management, Southwest Forestry University. His research and teaching interests cover international business, e-commerce, cross-cultural management, digital economy, and AI-enabled business innovation. His work combines academic inquiry with entrepreneurial and industry practice.

2011–2016 同济大学，应用数学/工商管理，学士 Tongji University, B.S. in Applied Mathematics / Business Administration
2019–2021 易三仓大学（AU），工商管理硕士 Assumption University (AU), MBA
2021–2024 国立发展管理学院（NIDA），哲学博士（管理学） National Institute of Development Administration (NIDA), Ph.D. in Management

Cross-border E-commerce

Studying AI adoption, platforms, content recommendation, livestreaming commerce, and cross-border digital trade.

国际商贸与跨文化管理

International Business & Cross-cultural Management

Researching Belt and Road business expansion, Yunnan enterprises in Southeast Asia, and cross-cultural collaboration.

AI商业模式创新

AI-enabled Business Model Innovation

结合教学与实践，探索AI技术在直播、电商运营、品牌管理和企业数字化转型中的应用路径。

Exploring how AI can be applied in livestreaming, e-commerce operations, brand management, and digital transformation.

教学与指导强调“理论框架—案例分析—项目实践—成果表达”的连续训练，结合职业规划、创新创业竞赛、电商直播实战等场景，帮助学生形成问题识别、方案设计、团队协作与商业表达能力。

My teaching emphasizes an integrated pathway from theoretical frameworks to case analysis, project-based practice, and outcome presentation. Through career planning, entrepreneurship competitions, and e-commerce livestreaming practice, students are guided to develop problem identification, solution design, teamwork, and business communication skills.
